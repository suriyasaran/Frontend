A racing bike is a high-performance motorcycle designed for speed and agility on paved roads, featuring a lightweight frame and powerful engine

A mountain cycle, also known as a mountain bike (MTB), is designed for off-road cycling. It features a sturdy frame, wide, knobby tires for traction on uneven terrain, and a suspension system to absorb shocks from rough trails

A cruiser bike, also known as a beach cruiser, is a bicycle that typically features a classic design with a curved frame, wide handlebars, and balloon tires. These bikes are designed for casual riding and comfort, often with a relaxed, upright seating position.

A racing bike, also known as a road bike, is a bicycle designed for speed and efficiency on paved roads. These bikes are characterized by their lightweight frames, drop handlebars, narrow tires, and multiple gears.
